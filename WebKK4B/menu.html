Drink

Milk Shake
sweet and cold drinks which are usually made from ice cream, hot milk or cold milk. combined with flavor powder or syrup Which is made by shaking (shake) using a shaker, or blended using a blender machine

Ice Blended
Ice cubes that are crushed until smooth usually use a blender machine, and mixed with coffee, flavor powder or flavored syrup, so as to create a delicious taste and give a cold sensation.

Smoothie
A fresh drink with the same characteristics as an ice blend, which is ice that is crushed until smooth, but uses vegetable and real fruit as the base ingredient.

Fruit Tea
A tea drink mixed with fruit juice syrup / concentrate to flavor the tea. usually the syrup used is not too thick (weak taste), so as not to remove the aroma from the tea itself.
